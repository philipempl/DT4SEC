## Working title: Digital Twins for IoT/CPS Cyber Security: a Survey.
## ACM Computing Surveys (CSUR)

## Goal & RQ

### Research Goal
- Find out the scenarios in which the Digital Twin is used in the security context for the Internet of Things/ Cyber-physical Systems

### Research Questions
- In which IoT/CPS domains is a digital twin used to improve cybersecurity?
- To what extent does the digital twin influence or enhance cybersecurity in IoT/CPS?
- Which methods or modes are used specifically?
- What is the architecture or how are digital twins properly embedded and integrated in IoT/CPS environments?
- DT Governance?
